Ai Fraud Surveying Tool

Research Prototype

Can you spot AI images in a swipe challenge?

This prototype was developed by Dr. Kara Hosford and asks you to classify visual content as AI-generated or real. Your responses are anonymous and all data is saved localy on your device.

  • Swipe left for AI-generated.
  • Swipe right for real content.
  • Desktop users can click the action buttons.
  • Your results are shown at the end, including an overall ranking estimate.

Information & Consent

This is a prototype and subject to potential bugs. Participation is voluntary. You can stop at any point by closing the page. Data is stored localy in your browser..
